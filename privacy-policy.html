Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

Introduction

Aurora Content Manager ("the App") is a personal content management tool used to publish content to Instagram and Facebook on behalf of its owner. This policy describes how data is handled.

Data Collection

The App accesses the following data through Facebook and Instagram APIs:

  • Public profile information (name, profile picture)
  • Facebook Pages managed by the user
  • Instagram Business Account information

This data is used solely for the purpose of publishing content to the connected Instagram and Facebook accounts.

Data Storage

Access tokens are stored securely on a private server. No user data is shared with third parties. No data is sold or used for advertising purposes.

Data Sharing

The App does not share any user data with third parties.

Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as necessary to provide the App's functionality. Users may request deletion of their data at any time.
